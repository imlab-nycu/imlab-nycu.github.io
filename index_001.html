Welcome to IM Lab

We are dedicated to advancing research in robotics, microfluidics, and plasma. Our mission is to develop cutting-edge microsystems for automation, biomedical applications, and advanced manufacturing.

Our Research

  • Robotics: Vision-based sensing, manipulation, and automation.
  • Microfluidics: Droplet generation, bioengineering applications, and lab-on-a-chip technology.
  • Plasma: Surface modification, thin-film deposition, and energy applications.
